2. **DNS-based Authentication of Named Entities (DANE)**: DANE is a security protocol that aims  to enhance the trustworthiness of digital certificates used in secure internet communications. By binding digital certificates to DNS records, DANE  eliminates  the need  for traditional certificate authorities reducing the risk of certificate fraud.
