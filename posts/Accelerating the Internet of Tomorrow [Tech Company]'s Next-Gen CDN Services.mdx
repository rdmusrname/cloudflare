The development of CDN edge  infrastructure has been instrumental in shaping the next generation of CDN services. CDNs have traditionally relied on a network of servers strategically placed around the world to deliver content efficiently. However, with the rise of edge computing, CDNs are now leveraging edge infrastructure to bring content closer to the end-users.

One notable example of this is Fornax a next-generation edge computing platform.
